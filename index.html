ABOUT NCR REGIMENT

NCR Regiment is a Level 7 Free Fire Guild based in Delhi, India. Built on teamwork, loyalty and competitive spirit, our guild unites 55 dedicated players under one banner. We focus on Guild Wars, Rank Push, Custom Room Battles and helping every member improve their gameplay. Our goal is not only to win matches but also to build a strong brotherhood where every member is valued. "One Guild. One Family. One Mission."
